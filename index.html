Road Map

Phase 0: Just the start -

We’ll giveaway 500 whitelist spots to members in our discord community.

Phase 1: Release -

Cool Shibas minting will be open on our minting website. We’ll reach out to different influencers to promote our project.

Phase 2: 25% minted -

We'll create a DAO wallet and holders will have a say how much ETH will go into the wallet, We'll put 5 ETH of our own into the wallet. Along with that, we will be doing multiple ETH giveaways for holders and non-holders.

Phase 3: 50% minted -

We'll giveaway 20 Cool Shiba NFTs. Along with that we will also be giving away $25,000 to a charity chosen by the community.

Phase 4: 100% minted -

We will give away a total of $25,000 in giveaways to our holders. We’ll also drop our Cool Shibas merch line to all holders. We’ll also be giving $100,000 to a charity of community’s choice.
